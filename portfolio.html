About Me

I am a versatile Product Manager with 6 years of experience in successfully leading the design and development of software applications. I have been involved in the product lifecycle from conception to launch. I am passionate about every aspect of software development.
"Customer Development" is the ideology I follow for software development. I take efforts to understand my customer's pain points and I strive to provide them with the solution that ehy expect from me and the one that best suits their needs. I believe in giving customers what they ant rather than enforcing my solutions on them. At the end I want them to buy my product and be highly satisfied with it.
In my spare time I think of day to day applications we use and I try to come up with my top requirements from such applications and then I try to design them as if I am the product manager of that software product. In my spare time I write blogs on Quora about product designing.
Please check my projects on this website to see my work in action!
